**Files:**
- Components: PascalCase (`UserProfile.tsx`)
- Utilities: camelCase (`formatDate.ts`)
- Constants: UPPER_SNAKE_CASE (`API_ENDPOINTS.ts`)

**Variables:**
- camelCase for variables
- PascalCase for types/interfaces
- UPPER_SNAKE_CASE for constants
